Google Drive Untrasher

This single page app will automatically (in fact it's probably already started) untrash all of your Google Drive files. First time through you will need to give it permission to access your files. Once you're done, you should totally go into your Google account settings at https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions?pli=1 and revoke permission!

If you don't see the authorization popup, check that that it hasn't been blocked by browser settings or anti-virus s/w
